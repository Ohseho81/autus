# The AUTUS Manifesto

## We Believe
1. AI should belong to people, not corporations
2. Privacy is structure, not feature
3. AI should learn YOU, not everyone
4. You deserve the best AI, always
5. AI should get better with you

## We Reject
- Surveillance capitalism
- Centralized control
- Generic responses
- Vendor lock-in
- Stagnant systems

## We Build
- AI Sovereignty
- Structural Privacy
- Personal Intelligence
- Provider Agnostic
- Self-Improvement

## The Five Principles
I. Zero Identity
II. Privacy by Architecture
III. Meta-Circular Development
IV. Minimal Core, Infinite Extension
V. Network Effect as Moat

## The Vision: Air-Like Monopoly
Everywhere, Essential, Invisible, Unownable, Irreplaceable

The future starts now.
